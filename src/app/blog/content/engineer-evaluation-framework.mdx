This framework defines expectations for software engineers across five levels: Junior, Mid, Senior, Lead, and Principal. It covers three disciplines: Direction, Engineering, and Approach.

I used it in my role at 1KOMMA5° to evaluate my own performance as a software engineer and to give feedback to my reports.

## <Blink color="#3b82f6">▌</Blink> AI

      <TableHead>Expectation</TableHead>
    </TableRow>
  </TableHeader>
  <TableBody>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell className="align-top">Junior</TableCell>
      <TableCell><ul className="my-0!"><li>Uses AI tools (copilot, chat) daily for code generation, debugging, learning</li><li>Knows when AI output needs review</li></ul></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell className="align-top">Mid</TableCell>
      <TableCell><ul className="my-0!"><li>Integrates AI into personal workflow systematically</li><li>Writes effective prompts, builds small automations</li><li>Evaluates AI output critically</li></ul></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell className="align-top">Senior</TableCell>
      <TableCell><ul className="my-0!"><li>Drives AI adoption within team</li><li>Identifies high-leverage use cases (code review, testing, docs, incident triage)</li><li>Sets quality bar for AI-assisted output</li><li>Builds reusable prompts or workflows for the team</li></ul></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell className="align-top">Lead</TableCell>
      <TableCell><ul className="my-0!"><li>Defines AI strategy for the domain</li><li>Evaluates build-vs-buy for AI tooling</li><li>Ensures AI usage aligns with data privacy, cost, and reliability constraints</li><li>Scales what works across teams</li></ul></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell className="align-top">Principal</TableCell>
      <TableCell><ul className="my-0!"><li>Shapes org-wide AI strategy</li><li>Defines where human-in-the-loop is non-negotiable</li><li>Drives competitive advantage through AI-native workflows</li><li>Owns cost/value framework for AI investments</li></ul></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableBody>
</Table>
