This page describes strategies to get into focus mode and to stay focused.

### **Preparation**

- ⏰  Time: **Decide on a time span** how long you want to stay in focus ([timeboxing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeboxing))
- 📑  Resources: **Prepare resources** for the session (PDF, code)
- 📱 **Phone**: Put your **phone on focus mode** ([iOS](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT212608), [Android](https://www.android.com/digital-wellbeing/#dwb-focus-mode)), Switch **Wi-Fi off** (if possible)
- 📍 **Location**: Be in an **Area with minimal distractions**
- 💧  **Vitals**: Prepare **Water and Snacks** (so you don't need to break)
- 🎧  **Music**: Listen to **concentration music** (no vocals, no strong melody switches)
    1. Brown Noise ([Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/2BhHUEJejfxgghJQdbimmj?si=7ad5fd05f08440fd), [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RqzGzwTY-6w))
    2. Alpha Waves ([Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/album/3g7XNBeCVOZiBx325CvbR1?si=2srnQ2NsSBStRKAonHHKAw), [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WPni755-Krg))
- 📺  **Watch**: “Study together” through video (["study with me" on Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=study+with+me))

### **How to start**

- Count down 5 seconds and start ([The 5 second rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Five-second_rule))
- Start with the first 5 min of work (you'll probably do it longer)
- Use a Promodoro Timer for difficult tasks ([Pomodoro Technique](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique))

### **Take effective breaks**

- ☕️  Brew coffee or tea.
- 😮‍💨  Do a breathing exercise or meditate.
- 🪟  Stare out of a window.
- 📗  Read a page of a book or a blog post.This can be a good substitute for social media checking.
- 🧹 Tidy up a part of your room.
- 💤  Lie down with your eyes closed or nap.
- 🙆‍♀️  Stand up and stretch.
- 🚶 Take a short walk
    - [Studies](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5981243/) say that taking a walk through nature boosts your ability to concentrate by as much as 20 % and at the same time reduces your stress levels.
- 💬  Have a chat with a roommate or a colleague.
- 📞  Call someone.
- 🏋️  Do a quick workout.
- 🚿  Take a shower.
- 🥗  Eat a healthy snack.
- ✍️  Doodle.
